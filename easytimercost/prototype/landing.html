FAQ

Questions contractors ask us.

Does AI actually send money / contracts / stuff without me clicking?
Only for things you explicitly allow. Default is "propose → you approve." You can set thresholds (e.g. auto-approve expenses under $500). Every auto-action is logged. Turn off anything anytime.
What if AI is wrong?
Every action is reversible. Every decision has an "explain this" button — you see exactly what data it used. Our Compliance Watchdog monitors error rates per agent and pauses them if they drift. Worst case: you hit undo and it's like it never happened.
Is my data safe?
Firestore (Google Cloud) · US or EU residency option · encrypted at rest · tenant isolation enforced at DB level · SOC 2 Type II in progress (target Q3 2026). We don't train models on your data. Period.
My crew doesn't speak English well / isn't tech-savvy
Worker AI talks in WhatsApp/Telegram/SMS in their native language (Spanish, Russian, Portuguese supported day 1 — more on request). The UI is just "type yes or send a photo." No buttons to learn.
Can I import from QuickBooks / TSheets?
Yes — one-time migration included with Pro and Scale. Starter can self-import via CSV. We support QB Online, QB Desktop, TSheets, Buildertrend, ServiceTitan, and generic CSV.
How fast do I see value?
Expense OCR saves hours in week 1. Time-tracking pain gone by week 2. Sales brief is the "wow" moment — first real call where AI tells you what they'll ask (usually within 10 days). Payroll savings show up at first period close.
What if I have 2 workers today and want to grow to 40?
All plans scale linearly. Add users anytime, pro-rated. Pro plan has unlimited users. Your AI agents get smarter with scale, not slower.
Can I cancel?
Yes. Monthly plans cancel anytime, no fees. Annual plans refunded pro-rata if you cancel within 60 days. You own your data — full export (CSV + PDF) available forever.
